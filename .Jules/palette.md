## 2025-02-09 - [Keyboard Shortcuts & A11y Polish]
**Learning:** Adding keyboard shortcut hints (e.g., [n]) alongside numerical navigation greatly improves discoverability of shortcuts in interactive narrative apps. Using aria-live='polite' for loading states ensures screen reader users are aware of async transitions without being interrupted.
**Action:** Always include visual hints for keyboard shortcuts and use aria-live regions for dynamic content loading in future player components.
